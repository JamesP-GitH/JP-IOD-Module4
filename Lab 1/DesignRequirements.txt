Target audience: 
Horror fans who want to share thoughts, reviews, fan art, movie clips, etc.

Content type: 
Mostly text posts, images, and maybe short video clips

Core features:
- Posting (text, images, videos)
- Viewing posts from others (feed)
- Liking / reacting
- Commenting on posts
- Profiles with user’s favorite movies, bio, posts

Unique niche feature ideas:
-“Scare Meter” — rate posts or movies on a horror scale (1 to 10 chills)
-Horror trivia or polls in the feed
-“Watchlist” — add movies you want to watch

Tone and style: 
Dark theme with blood red, eerie shadows, maybe some subtle horror motifs.
